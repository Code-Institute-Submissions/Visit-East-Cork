Midleton
Show More

Midleton is a beautiful market town in the heart of East Cork. It is famous for the renowned Midleton Distillery, home of Jameson Whiskey and its premium brand Midleton Rare. Midleton is also home to a country market which is held every Saturday and attracts local agricultutral and artisan producers who sell their products direct to the customer.

Cobh
Show More

Cobh is a spectacular coastal town on an island in East Cork. It is a popular destination for Cruise Ships and was the last port of call of the infamous Titanic. A visit to the Titanic museum is a must on a visit to this pretty town. Cobh is also home to St. Coleman's Cathedral which sits high above the town with incredible views of the Cork estuary and out to sea. Spike Island is an awars winning historic prison accessed by boat from Cobh pier, this journey will provide you with up close views of Ireland's Naval Base on Haulbowline Island.

Castlemartyr
Show More

Castlemartyr is a scenic rural village in East Cork. It is famous for the beautiful Castlemartyr Resort including an 18 hotel parkland golf course and a luxurious spa. The area around Castlemartyr is lush and green with some of the best farmland in Ireland. A visit to Ballymaloe House Hotel and Cookery School in nearby Shanag arry is not to be missed
